My Story


I'm Justyn and I created Justyn Jen to inspire and teach others about traveling alone, traveling full time and freelancing to fund your travels. I have been on the road for over 3 years now, visiting over 50 countries on 6 continents. I started freelancing about 2 years ago when my savings ran out but I wasn't ready to go home. I found myself broke and alone on the other side of the world from my family and I had to learn and start working very quickly if I was going to survive.

Now I work as an online marketer helping bloggers and business owners grow their traffic through Pinterest focussed marketing

If you're interested in growing your business and site through Pinterest, please get in touch!
